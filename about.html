About Me

I received a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Delaware and an M.S. and D.Sc. in Computer Science from Washington University in St. Louis. I have been on the faculties at Indiana University, the Ohio State University, Florida Southern College, and currently, the University of Wisconsin - La Crosse.

I enjoy hiking in the mountains (not in Florida) and photography, hence the banner photos on this site. My sons and I are fans of The Grand Tour (formerly Top Gear) and English Premiere League football.
